Before You Start

  • Camera on tripod, phone PORTRAIT (vertical) — the app is mobile, videos play in a portrait bottom sheet
  • Front-lit or 45-degree lighting — no backlighting
  • Warm up before filming
  • Use 60-70% of working weight (looks real, perfect form)
  • 2-3 clean reps per clip, then optionally 1 "wrong" rep
  • Review each clip before moving on
  • 15-20 seconds per exercise
