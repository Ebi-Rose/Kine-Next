Progress · A+B × Personalization Engine

The combined A+B layout (hero card · tabs · dashboard grid · depth reads) rendered for all 8 archetypes. Same skeleton, eight different stories — each driven by computeProgressLayout(profile, history).

Reading guide. Each row shows the engine's reasoning on the left (which rules fired, which cards are shown or hidden) and the rendered combined-layout phone on the right. The hero card always carries the headline verdict; the tabs always exist (Strength/Body/History or Training/Rehab/History depending on goal); the grid tiles and depth cards change per profile. Personalization is silent — the only tell is the single line under "Progress".

What to notice. Same hero+tabs+grid+depth skeleton in every phone — but the hero's verdict changes from "Stronger by 8%" to "12 sessions in" to "Holding steady" to "Lower body holding · upper on pause", the grid tiles swap content, the tabs adapt their labels (Strength/Body/History → Training/Rehab/History when there's active rehab work), and the depth cards reflect what actually matters for that profile. No user is ever told something was hidden. Every card is reachable via "What I want to see" if they go looking. That's what principles #1, #2, #6 and #19 look like as a real screen.
