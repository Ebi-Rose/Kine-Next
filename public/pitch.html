Built for women who train with intention

Primary audience

Women aged 22–40 who want to strength train consistently but feel overwhelmed by programming. You’ve tried apps like Hevy or Strong but felt unsupported. You don’t want to pay £200+/month for a personal trainer. You want your body acknowledged — cycle, conditions, life stage — without gimmicks.

Education-motivated

Women who don’t just want to be told what to do — you want to understand why. You research training online, follow evidence-based creators, but can’t translate knowledge into a structured programme. You see a training tool as a bridge to independence: get stronger and smarter, until you don’t need us at all.

Returning and adapting

Women returning to training postpartum. Women in perimenopause looking for adapted programming. Experienced lifters who want smarter periodisation. Your body has changed — your training should know that.
