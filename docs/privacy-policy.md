# Privacy Policy

**Kine ("we", "us", "our")**
**Last updated: March 2026**

Kine is a strength training app built for women. We take the privacy of your personal and health-related data seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

---

## 1. What data we collect

### Account data
- Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
- Name (optional, for personalisation)

### Personal profile data
- Date of birth, height, weight, location
- Training age (how long you've been training)

### Training data
- Goal, experience level, available equipment
- Training schedule and session durations
- Session logs: exercises performed, sets, reps, weights
- Weekly feedback: effort and soreness ratings, notes
- Lift history and personal records
- Injury and limitation selections

### Cycle and hormonal data
- Cycle type (regular, irregular, hormonal contraception, perimenopause, or not applicable)
- Period start dates (if you choose to provide them)
- Average cycle length

This is classified as **sensitive health data** under GDPR and similar regulations. We collect it only with your explicit consent and use it solely to adapt your training program.

### Technical data
- Device type, browser, and operating system (for compatibility)
- Error logs and crash reports (anonymised)

### Payment data
- Subscription status and billing history are managed by **Stripe**. We do not store your card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers.

---

## 2. How we use your data

| Purpose | Data used |
|---|---|
| Build your weekly training program | Goal, experience, equipment, schedule, duration, injuries, cycle phase |
| Adapt programs over time | Session logs, weekly feedback, lift progression |
| Cycle-aware adjustments | Cycle type, period dates, cycle length |
| Account management | Email, name |
| Process payments | Managed by Stripe (see their privacy policy) |
| Improve the app | Anonymised usage patterns, error logs |

We do **not** use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with other users.

---

## 3. AI processing

Kine uses AI (Anthropic's Claude) to generate your training programs and post-session coaching. When this happens:

- Your training profile (goal, experience, equipment, schedule, injuries, cycle phase) is sent to the AI to generate personalised programming.
- Session performance data is sent for post-session analysis.
- **Your name, email, and payment information are never sent to the AI.**
- AI-generated content is not used to train AI models (per Anthropic's API data policy).

---

## 4. Where your data is stored

- **Supabase** (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS) — Your account and training data
- **Local device storage** — A copy of your data for offline access
- **Stripe** — Payment and subscription data
- **Vercel** — App hosting (no persistent user data stored)

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

---

## 5. Data retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.
- Anonymised, aggregated data (e.g., average session durations across all users) may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

---

## 6. Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — View everything we store about you from your profile.
- **Export** your data — Request a full export of your training history and profile.
- **Correct** your data — Update your profile at any time within the app.
- **Delete** your data — Delete your account and all associated data.
- **Withdraw consent** — Stop sharing cycle data at any time by changing your cycle type to "not applicable."

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **[privacy@kinefit.app]**.

---

## 7. Cookies and tracking

Kine does not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking pixels. We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management.
