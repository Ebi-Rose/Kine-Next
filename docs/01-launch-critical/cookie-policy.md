# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## The Short Version

Kinē does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. We use browser localStorage for app functionality only.

## What We Use

| Technology | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| **localStorage** | Cache training data for offline use, store UI preferences, session state | Until you clear browser data or delete your account |
| **Supabase auth token** | Keep you logged in between visits | Until you log out or token expires |
| **Service worker cache** | Enable offline PWA functionality | Managed automatically by the browser |

## What We Don't Use

- No third-party tracking cookies
- No analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No advertising cookies or pixels
- No social media tracking widgets
- No fingerprinting scripts

## Third-Party Services

- **Stripe** may set its own cookies during the checkout process for fraud prevention. See [Stripe's cookie policy](https://stripe.com/cookies-policy).
- **Supabase** authentication uses localStorage tokens, not cookies.

## Your Control

You can clear all Kinē data from your browser at any time:
1. Open your browser settings
2. Clear site data for kinefit.app
3. This will log you out and remove cached data (your account data remains safe on our servers)
