## Never

Things we've deliberately decided not to do.

| Feature | Why Not |
|---|---|
| Social feed / followers | Principle 3 (Closed Loop). Social comparison harms our target users. |
| Leaderboards / challenges | Principle 14 (No Scores). Gamification creates anxiety. |
| Before/after photos | Appearance-focused framing. We're about capability. |
| Calorie tracking | Out of scope. Different product category with different expertise required. |
| Wearable data import | Principle 3. Self-report is the signal. Wearable data for strength is unreliable. |
| Ads / sponsored content | Conflicts with trust. Revenue comes from subscriptions. |
| Marketplace (sell programs) | Creates influencer dynamics we're avoiding. |
| AI chatbot ("talk to your coach") | AI is infrastructure (Principle 8). Users interact with programs, not a chatbot. |

## How Features Move Through the Roadmap

1. **Idea surfaces** (user request, founder idea, competitive pressure)
2. **Principles check** — does it align with or violate any principles?
3. **Effort/impact assessment** — how much work vs how much user value?
4. **Scope definition** — what's the minimum version that delivers value?
5. **Build → Ship → Measure** — did it actually help?
6. **Iterate or cut** — if it didn't help, remove it
